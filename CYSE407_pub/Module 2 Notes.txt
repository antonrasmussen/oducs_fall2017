Important things to remember for aquring evidence -- first time probably won't go right

stay current on rel legislation

Decide where evidence is located, direct investigation that way

Try not to alter data source

Evidence presented is derived from data on source drive

Perform analysis on copy, not original--make more than one copy usually, hashes should be the same

Document actions

Note: If you don't write it down, you cannot verify that you did something or how you did it. If your notes have errors or omissions, it calls your entire report into question.
